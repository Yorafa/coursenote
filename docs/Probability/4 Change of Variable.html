Discrete​

Let XXX be a discrete random variable with PMF pXp_XpX​. Let Y=h(X),h:R→RY=h(X), h:\R\to \RY=h(X),h:R→R is some function. Then YYY is also discrete with PMF pY(y)=∑x∈h−1(y)pX(x)p_Y(y)= \sum_{x\in h^{-1}(y)}p_X(x)pY​(y)=∑x∈h−1(y)​pX​(x)
