Multivariate Normal Distribution

Key Properties​

Suppose XXX follows the multivariate normal distribution with mean vector μ\mathbf{\mu}μ and variance-covariance matrix Σ\mathbf{\Sigma}Σ, then:

  1. Linear combinations of the components of XXX are normally distributed.
  2. All subsets of XXX are also multivariate normal distributed.
  3. Zero covariance means implies independent.
  4. The conditional distributions of the components is still multivariate normal distributed.
