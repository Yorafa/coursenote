Fundamental Theorem of Finite Abelian Groups

Fundamental Theorem of Finite Abelian Groups states that: every finite Abelian group is a direct product of cyclic groups of prime power order. Moreover, the number of terms in the product and the orders of the cyclic groups are uniquely determined by the group.

Fundamental Theorem of Finite Abelian Groups​

Every finite generated abelian group GGG is isomorphic to a direct product of cyclic groups. i.e. G≅Zp1n1×⋯×Zpknk×ZbG\cong \Z_{p_1^{n_1}} \times \cdots \times \Z_{p_k^{n_k}}\times \Z^bG≅Zp1n1​​​×⋯×Zpknk​​​×Zb where pip_ipi​ are prime numbers and nin_ini​ are positive integers.

  • We call bbb the Betti number of GGG. A finite generated abelian group is finite if and only if its Betti number is zero.
