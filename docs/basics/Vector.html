Vector

we first define vector which is a collection of data in n×1n\times1n×1 as [x1x2x3⋮xn]\begin{bmatrix}x_1\\ x_2\\x_3\\\vdots\\x_n\end{bmatrix}​x1​x2​x3​⋮xn​​​, and matrix is a collection of data in n×pn\times pn×p as [x11x12⋯x1px21x22⋯x2p⋯⋯⋯⋯xn1xn2⋯xnp]\begin{bmatrix}x_{11} & x_{12} & \cdots & x_{1p} \\ x_{21} & x_{22} & \cdots & x_{2p} \\ \cdots & \cdots & \cdots & \cdots \\x_{n1} & x_{n2} & \cdots & x_{np}\end{bmatrix}​x11​x21​⋯xn1​​x12​x22​⋯xn2​​⋯⋯⋯⋯​x1p​x2p​⋯xnp​​​
