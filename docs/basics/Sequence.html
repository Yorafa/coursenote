Monotone Sequence​

A sequence {an}n≥1\{a_n\}_{n\ge1}{an​}n≥1​, if an≤an+1a_n\le a_{n+1}an​≤an+1​, then it's increasing. If an≥an+1a_n\ge a_{n+1}an​≥an+1​, then it's decreasing. A sequence either increase or decrease is a monotone sequence.

  • A not monotone sequence example {an}n≥1\{a_n\}_{n\ge1}{an​}n≥1​, where an=(−1)nna_n = (-1)^nnan​=(−1)nn

Monotone Convergence Theorem:

  • A monotone increasing sequence and bounded above converges to its supremum.
  • A monotone decreasing sequence and bounded below converges to its infimum.

Bolzano-Weierstrass Theorem: Every bounded sequence of real numbers has a convergent subsequence
