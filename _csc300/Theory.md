## Virtue(美德) Ethics

Ethics(伦理): the term describes the investigation(调查) and analysis of moral(道德) principles and dilemmas(困境). Or the rules/standard/guidelines that establish what conduct(行为/执行) is right and wrong for individuals and groups. Often use to refer personal beliefs.

-   Meta: deals with the foundations and nature of moral values, properties and words
-   Normative: deals with systems of morality and questions how one ought to be and act morally.
-   Applied: deals with what a person is premitted to do in specific situations

Virtue Ethics: a philosophy developed by Aristotle and others; helps people understand what it means to be a virtuous human being
